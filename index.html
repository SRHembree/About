I am Sydney, a UX/UI Designer from Atlanta.

I have a background in marketing, broadcast journalism, graphic design, and nonprofit management. My passions include solving user pain points and tackling challenges in innovative ways. With experiences as a team leader, designer, and front-end coder, I would love to take on your head-scratchers!

I graduated with dual degrees in marketing and broadcast journalism from the University of Mississippi and followed the experience up with a Masters in Public Administration from Kennesaw State University. Since then, I attended and recently completed the Georgia Institute of Technology's UX/UI Bootcamp. Through this program, I worked alongside the Streams of Sound nonprofit based in Jacksonville, Florida, and the Little Five Points Center for Arts & Community in Atlanta, Georgia, to improve their respective website usabilities.

Because travel has been a part of my life since the beginning, moving to Canada and Greece for a year and visiting over 26 countries across five continents, I am able to adapt to hurdles with little warning. I enjoy Studio Ghibli movies, singing along to showtunes, and Tex-Mex food because, in my mind, nothing is as satisfying after working hard than tacos.
