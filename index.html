I am Sydney, a Product Designer from Atlanta.

I have a background in marketing, broadcast journalism, graphic design, and nonprofit management. My passions include solving user pain points and tackling challenges in innovative ways. With experiences as a team leader, designer, and front-end coder, I would love to take on your head-scratchers!

I have a Bachelors in Marketing and Broadcast Journalism from the University of Mississippi and a Masters in Public Administration from Kennesaw State University. I have partnered with several nonprofits over the years to improve their websites' Usability, including the Streams of Sound (FL), the Little Five Points Center for Arts & Community (GA), and the Cobb Master Gardeners (GA).

Traveling is a hobby of mine and has been a part of my life since the beginning. Living in Canada and Greece for a time, as well as visiting over 26 countries across five continents, I am able to adapt to hurdles with little warning. I enjoy Studio Ghibli movies, singing along to showtunes, and Tex-Mex food because, in my mind, nothing is as satisfying after working hard than tacos.
